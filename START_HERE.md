## ❓ QUICK FAQ

**Q: Do I need a database?**  
A: No! Everything local. Data goes to browser storage.

**Q: Is my data safe?**  
A: Yes! Never leaves your device. You control it.

**Q: Can I lose data?**  
A: Only if you clear browser storage. Download Excel backup weekly.

**Q: Can I share my list?**  
A: Yes! Export as Excel and send to friends.

**Q: Will it work offline?**  
A: Yes! Fully offline-capable (except image loading).

**Q: Can I deploy it?**  
A: Yes! See DEPLOY.md for easy deployment.

**Q: Can I modify it?**  
A: Absolutely! It's open and yours to customize.

**Q: What if I find a bug?**  
A: Report it in CONTRIBUTING.md or fix it yourself!
